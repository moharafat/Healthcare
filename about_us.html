About This Project

This project was inspired by our collective desire to create a healthier, more connected community. It all started when one of our team members experienced a personal health challenge that highlighted the importance of accessible health information and resources. We realized there was a gap in the market for a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that could provide this crucial support.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, which has provided us with the skills and knowledge to bring our vision to life.
